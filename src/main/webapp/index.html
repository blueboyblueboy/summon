What is Summon the Screensaver?
  • Summon the Screensaver is a powerful but compact freeware which enables you to invoke and start the screensaver or lock the workstation instantly without clicking your mouse or pressing short-cut keys. It also enables you to temporarily disable the screensaver.
 
Features
  • Invokes the screensaver or locks the workstation instantly without the need of complex key/mouse inputs. Summon the Screensaver becomes handy in situations where you are not using your PC for a while and you do not want others to use it or see the stuff opened on the screen, or you just want your screensaver to appear immediately when you leave for lunch. Just place the mouse cursor at one of the corners of your desktop, say some magic words in your heart and your screensaver or lock-screen will be summoned immediately onto your screen!


  • Disables the screensaver at any time you want, for example, while watching movies/animations in window mode, playing games in window mode with a joypad, long-duration installation, up/downloading big files, burning disc.


  • Detects, in real-time, the state of the screensaver automatically, so that you could know whether the screensaver is active or has been temporarily disabled by some programs.
